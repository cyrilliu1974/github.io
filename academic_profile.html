About Me

I explore the intersection of artificial intelligence, symbolic representation, and emergent communication in multi-agent systems. My research focuses on developing discrete communication frameworks that enable AI agents to learn interpretable and semantically grounded languages through reinforcement learning and vector quantization techniques.

Recent Preprint

Title:
Emergent Discrete Language for Multi-Agent Coordination via VQ-VAE and Communication-Aware Learning

Abstract:
We present a novel architecture for learning discrete communication protocols in multi-agent environments. By leveraging VQ-VAE for symbol grounding and introducing communication-aware reinforcement objectives, our system demonstrates structured language emergence that supports coordination and symbolic reasoning.

Keywords: VQ-VAE, Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning, Emergent Language, Symbolic Communication, AI Mother Tongue
