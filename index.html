Welcome to The Glasses Dog

The Glasses Dog is an independent workflow automation platform powered by n8n, designed to help individuals and businesses streamline processes and boost productivity.

Our platform seamlessly integrates with a variety of Google services, such as Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Drive, and more. By leveraging n8n's flexible automation tools, you can easily create workflows that:

  • Automatically send and manage emails via Gmail.
  • Schedule and update events in Google Calendar.
  • Sync and manage documents in Google Drive.
  • Automate data transfers between Google Sheets and other applications.

Much like how n8n enables users to combine different services into efficient automated processes, The Glasses Dog empowers you to harness the full potential of Google services. Our secure and scalable integrations help ensure that your workflows are not only efficient but also reliable.

Explore our services and discover how you can simplify your daily tasks and focus on what matters most—growing your business.
